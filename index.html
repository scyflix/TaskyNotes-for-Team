Why teams choose LogHue

LogHue gives structure to the way work moves from idea to completion, without adding noise or clutter.

Workspace centered

Create focused spaces for teams, projects, or departments and keep tasks, logs, and members together.

Task and progress tracking

Assign tasks, log what was done, add notes and dates, and see what is in progress or complete.

Clear roles

Admins guide the work, members focus on doing the work, and everyone sees what matters to them.

Calm and modern design

A dark, focused interface that feels familiar and stays out of the way of real work.

What you can do with LogHue

Create workspaces

Set up a workspace for each team or project and keep tasks, logs, and members in one place.

Assign tasks with clarity

Give people clear tasks with context so they know what to do and where to log their work.

Log real work

Team members record what they did, add notes, choose a date, and mark progress as in progress or complete.

Review and comment

Admins read logs, leave comments, and guide the work without constant calls or messages.

Track progress

See which tasks are active, which are waiting, and which are done across all workspaces.

Close tasks with confidence

When the work is complete, admins close the task so everyone knows it is finished.

A design team has a workspace. The lead creates a task for a new landing page. A designer logs what they did, adds notes, and marks the work as in progress.
